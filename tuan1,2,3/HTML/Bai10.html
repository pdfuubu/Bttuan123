IUH

Industrial University of Ho Chi Minh City was formerly Go Vap
Vocational School founded by Don Bosco priests on November 11, 1956 at Hanh Thong commune,
Go Vap District, Gia Dinh Province.
In 1968, the school was renamed as the Don Bosco Private Junior High School of Technology.
Until January 31, 1970 the school was upgraded to be the Don Bosco Private Junior High School of Technology,
shortly referred to as the Don Bosco High School of Engineering .
