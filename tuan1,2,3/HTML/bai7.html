A smile a facial expession formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the sides of the mouth. It is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some smiles inlcude a contraction of the muscles at the corner of the eyes, an action known as a "Duchene smile".Smiles performed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
